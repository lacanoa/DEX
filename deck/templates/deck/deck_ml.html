Mara

Out of sample accuracy: 58%

Our latest model is Mara. Mara is a Voting Classifier, she takes votes from three classification algorithms:

  • Support Vector Machine
  • Random Forest Classifier
  • K-Nearest-Neighbors

We trained Mara on 5 years of the following daily data:

  • RTS close price
  • Close price for 15 other securities (including BR, Si, VTBR, among others)
  • Open positions (of physical and legal entities) for RTS futures, puts, and calls
  • At the money implied volatility.
